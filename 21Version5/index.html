Overview

Twenty-One, headquartered in Singapore, specialises in the DISTRIBUTION of IoT products and CUSTOMISATION OF SOLUTIONS.

Since 2013, Twenty-One has participated in solutions planning for individuals and corporates. The company is a proud distributor of many strong brands in the IoT industry with footprints in Singapore, Malaysia, Hong Kong, and the Philippines.

With rising global concerns, Twenty-One aims to actively plan for solutions to ensure a sustainable world for generations to come.

OUR MISSION

DISTRIBUTION

We strive to bring the latest gadgets to satisfy like-minded enthusiasts, ensuring everyone can explore the convenience, security, and transformation from technological advancements.

SOLUTIONS PLANNING

We innovate and create solutions that scream convenience and flexibility. With a core emphasis on user needs, every solution is a personalised plan.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to be a globally competitive player in the IoT industry and build solutions addressing global concerns.

OUR VALUES & BELIEFS

Twenty-One aims to be the one-stop to all your problems, be it as a product distributor or solutions consultant.

From basic home security and lifestyle wants, to corporate regulatory requirements and work simplification, Twenty-One believes that there is always a difference that can be made through the use of technology. No problem is ever too hard for us to resolve.

Our team designs every plan as a personalised solution. Every plan is an unique effort necessary to resolve pain points.
